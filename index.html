HOW TO PLAY

Objective

You are just a mere pixel in a scanline, tasked with matching the colors of the pixels that precede you. Your goal is simple: synchronise with the rhythm of the scanline and create a harmonious cascade of colors.

As the scanline progresses, coloured pixels will appear one after another. Your job is to replicate the color of the pixel immediately preceding you. Time your actions carefully and stay in tune.

Controls

  • Use the numbers 1 to 5 on your keyboard to select the colour that matches the preceding pixel.

NOTE: At the start of the game, only colours 1 to 3 are available. Pay attention for when a new colour is unlocked!

But beware! Missing a color match comes with consequences. You'll also incur a penalty that pushes you closer to failure. The longer you struggle to find the correct color, the more the screen will fill with noise, obscuring your path to success. As you progress, the pace quickens, and the challenges intensify.
